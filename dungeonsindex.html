Minecraft Dungeons

Minecraft Dungeons is a Minecraft styled RPG Game.
There's other unique weapons not in original Minecraft such as spears, daggers.

Primary Weapons are the melee weapons can use with LMB as default keybindings.
Primary Weapons can be sorted with various topics: Range, Attack Speed, DPS etc.

Secondary Weapons are the ranged weapons can use with RMB as default keybindings.
Secondary Weapons are divided into 2 groups: Bows, and Crossbows.
Bows are charge-shooting type. Holding RMB will charge and release to shoot.
Crossbows are instant-fire type. Pressing RMB will shoot arrows instantly.
Holding RMB will rapid-fire in crossbow's fire speed.

Armors are the only one equipment in Dungeons, which increases max HP.
Some armors don't increase HP so much, but gives passive abilites.

Gadgets are active abilities by pressing number keys.
Gadgets have 2 types: instant active, and equipable.
Instant active gadgets are used instatly by pressing right keybinds.
Poison mushroom, Fishing rods etc. are included in instant active gadgets.
Equipable gadgets can stock ability and use it in other time.
Equipable gadgets are better for various tactics, but can't react sudden situations.

Unique items have better stats than common weapons and have passive abilities.
Unique items have own names, which means they don't follow the original items.

You can obtains items by playing dungeons or buying them with emeralds or golds.
Emeralds are the basic currency in Dungeons, which can buy items and random boxes.
Golds are the advanced currency, which can buy items with golden abilities.

Enchantment gives passive abilities.
Enchantment needs Enchant Points obtainable by level up.
Rare Enchantments needs more Enchant Points per upgrade, but gives stronger power.

Hidden Dungeons can be discovered by finding the scrolls in basic dungeons.
Hidden Dungeons gives better items than basic dungeons.

Ingame Loots are items but cannot equip or take after dungeon ends.
There are 3 types in Ingame Loots: Restore, Potions, and TNT.
Restore loots, such as bread, apple, porks, arrow bundles, gives regeneration effect.
Bread has lowest healings per tick, but lasts 30 seconds.
Apple has average healings per tick, and lasts about 15 seconds.
Porks only lasts 8 seconds, but has highest healings per tick.
Arrow bundles gives arrows.
Potions gives status effect to consumed player.
Strength Potion gives Strength effect, which increases melee damage.
Shadow Potion gives Invisiblity, which hides you for duration time or before attacking.
Swiftness Potions increases movement speed for duration time.
TNT is a TNT, can throw with Secondary Weapon key, will explode after 3 seconds.

Adventure Pass gives cosmetics and currency. Buying Pass will give more cosmetics.

Difficulty

Default

Basic gamemode.
Difficulty Scale: 1.0x to 9.9x
Squid Coast is only available in Default Difficulty.

Adventure

Bit harder gamemode. Unlocked by beating the Arch Illager in Default difficulty.
Difficulty Scale: 9.9x to 52.4x
Scythe, Soul Bow, and various Armors are unlocked in Adventure Difficulty.

Apocalypse

Challenging gamemode.
Difficulty Scale: 52.4x to 277.6x
Dark Armor series are unlocked in Apocalypse Difficulty.

Apocalypse Plus

Hardest gamemode. Not chosen in Difficulty Selection.
Difficulty Scale: 379.5x to 120492.1x
Only for Tryhards.
