Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox game which everyone can use and enjoy.
Minecraft has various gamemodes to enjoy.
Minecraft can be played with old versions. You can choose whatever you like the most.
Minecraft Snapshot/Preview is Minecraft version which is testing incoming features.
Resource Packs are a pack of textures that changes ingame textures.
Mods are a .jar extension file that adds more features to Minecraft.
Data Packs are a pack that changes Minecraft. Affects strong, but not like Mods.
Shader Packs are a type of resource packs but changes shadows, bloom etc.
Minecraft supports servers. You can play any of them.
Common users can also host servers. They usually requires 'Hamachi'.
Users can buy or get unique server address to play with friends or others.

Minecraft Bedrock Edition/Minecraft for Windows 10

Minecraft Bedrock Edition is combined version of Minecraft Pocket Edition and Minecraft Console Edition.
Almosts are same with Minecraft Java Edition, but somethings are different.
Minecraft for Windows 10 is Minecraft Bedrock Edition for Computers.

Minecraft Dungeons

Minecraft Dungeons is a RPG based at Minecraft. There are various weapons and items such as daggers, glaves, and scythes.
You can use 1 melee, 1 armor, 1 ranged, and 3 gadgets in a time. Your goal is to beat the Arch Illager.

Minecraft Legends

Minecraft Legends is a Strategy Game based at Minecraft. Piglins are invading Overworld and you have to stop them.
You can control your army to fight with you. You also can try versus mode to fight with other players.
